About Us

Based in Destin, Florida, Dcomforters' Kitchen is a family owned Nigerian Restaurant, focused not only on providing excellent services but also creating an unforgettable experience to the senses of our prestigious customers.

Our culinary artisans draw inspiration from Nigerian, Western African, and Intercontinental dishes and have a diverse menu which features a symphony of flavors meticulously crafted to tantalize your taste buds.

The kitchen has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a go-to place for a meal any time of the day.

Dcomforters' Kitchen is owned by a Nigerian couple, Israel and Precious, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of providing excellent services to the world around them.

Our goal is to remain at the forefront of providing excellent services to everyone who comes visiting us and ensure they have an experience that is beyond just suppressing their hunger with a meal.
